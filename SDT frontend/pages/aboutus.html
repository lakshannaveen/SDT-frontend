About Colombo Air Quality Dashboard

Our Mission

We provide accurate, real-time air quality information to help Colombo residents make informed decisions about their health and outdoor activities.

Key Features

  • 📊 Real-time AQI monitoring across Colombo
  • 🔔 Instant health alerts for poor air quality
  • 📈 Historical data
  • 🔍 Detailed pollutant breakdowns

Our Technology

We combine data from multiple monitoring stations to deliver the most accurate air quality information for Colombo.

Our Team

A dedicated group of environmental scientists, data analysts, and public health advocates working to make air quality data accessible to everyone.
